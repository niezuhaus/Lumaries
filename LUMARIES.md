# Lumaries

![Photo1.jpg](Photo1.jpg)

Lumaries is an interactive installation built around the concepts of memories and interaction. The observer will notice a laserdot on a screen that leaves a glowing trace behind. In front of the screen is the "projector" with a controller on top.   

The work consists of mainly 2 parts: a phosphorescent screen and a wooden box, that has a controller attached to it.
 and a wooden box that has a control attached to it




The installation in its core element consist of a phosphorescent screen, a uv laser module, 2 prisms 2 stepper motors and a halogen spot. All of it is being controlled by an Arduino Mega 2560.

### the screen

The screen is covered in a special paint made of a transparent acryl media mixed with a special pigment that is strontium aluminate doped with rare-earths elements. The pigment can store energy from uv light and will transmit this energy over time as green light
- 360 and 400 nm works best
- 405nm 5mW used in installation
